History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of software enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the premier technology conferences in the world.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation and foster collaboration in the tech community. We aim to connect professionals, students, and enthusiasts through learning and networking.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has featured some of the brightest minds in the tech world.

Ada Lovelace

Known as the first computer programmer, Ada's insights into algorithms laid the groundwork for modern computing.

Grace Hopper

A computing pioneer and U.S. Navy rear admiral, Hopper helped invent the first compiler and made programming languages more accessible.
